## Target Channels for Rank-1 Test

Priority order (based on data quality and phase consistency):
1. **hb(1P)π** - Cleanest two-peak structure, highest significance (16σ)
2. **hb(2P)π** - Clear structure, lower stats (5.6σ)
3. **Υ(2S)π** - Good resolution, clear peaks
4. **Υ(3S)π** - Small phase space but clean

Note: hb channels have relative phase ~180°, Υ channels have ~0°
